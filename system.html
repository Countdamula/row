Identity Anchors
Step 01. Identify your identity anchors — limiting beliefs you've carried for years that weigh you down. "I'm lazy." "I'm bad with money." These aren't facts. They're identities you've accepted.
Future Self Vision
Step 02. Create a compelling vision of your future self. Set a timer for 30 minutes. Describe your ideal week as if it were your life right now — not five or ten years from now.
Install The New Identity Through Action
Step 03. One hour of doing beats ten hours of planning. Challenge your identity through action — e.g. believe "I'm not charismatic"? Talk to everyone you meet.
